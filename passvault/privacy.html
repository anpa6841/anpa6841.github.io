PassVault Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 9th, 2025

PassVault is a Chrome extension that helps users manage and autofill their passwords securely. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through this policy.

1. Data Collection

PassVault does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All user credentials are stored securely in your browser using Chrome's storage.local API.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used solely for the functionality of the extension—specifically to autofill and manage login credentials.

3. Data Security

All data is stored locally and securely using Chrome's storage system. PassVault does not transmit any data to third-party services or remote servers.

4. Third-Party Access

No third parties have access to your data. PassVault operates entirely within your browser environment.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, we will update the effective date at the top of the page.

6. Contact
